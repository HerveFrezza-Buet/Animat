Smallbot
We provide a small khepera-like robot simulated under V-REP. It handles a pan-tilt video device. It can be handled by ROS.
  • smallbot.ttm
Thetopics are the following ones:
Name Message Direction Meaning
cmd_vel geometry_msgs/Twist subscribe Motion command
front sensor_msgs/Image publish Video sensor
camerapos sensor_msgs/Point publish The (pan,tilt,0) position of the camera. Angles are in degree.
pantilt sensor_msgs/Point subscribe Set the (pan,tilt,0) position of the camera. Angles are in degree.
dpantilt sensor_msgs/Point subscribe Modifies the position of the camera by adding (dpan,dtilt,0) to the current position. Angles are in degree.
Food and water areas
In the scene, there are specific areas where the robot can enter. When the robot is inside an area, a message is sent on a topic to tell the kind of area the robot is in.
Name Message Direction Meaning
area std_msgs/String publish When the robot is in an area, it published here its kind.
Food areas
The publish "food" on the topic.

Water areas
